## SD card track layout

- 001.mp3 – Boot
- 002.mp3 – Wake reply ("Hi Wall-E")
- 003–004 – Idle
- 005.mp3 – Curious
- 006.mp3 – E-stop
- **007.mp3 – 026.mp3** – Command reply sounds

Place your audio files in the SD root or `mp3/` folder as 001.mp3, 002.mp3, etc.
